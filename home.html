What will be featured in this website?

Our website seeks to understand and interpret art, by posting them on this website and demonstrating what they mean to us. Join us in understaing and interpreting a variety ofart types, regardless of their fame, but moreso because of the way they are percieved. Work beside art may also be interpretted, such as poems, songs, etc.

In order to create this website we used a variety of different techniques; however, that required us to do a variety of research. With this research me and my associate were also able to understand what others believed about the divergent types of art there are.

As creators of this website we believed it was important to add a variety of different art types; however, instead of adding every single image we will name, describe and interpret the art piece based on our perspective.
